Rules for Codemasters

  1. Access the board here
  2. The border color of the cards determines which team goes first.
  3. Help your team correctly select your 8 codewords using hints!
  4. You can't communicate with your team in any other way!
  5. Hints consist of:
    • One word that is related to your codewords (you can't use any part of the codeword as a hint!)
    • The number of words on the board that are connected to your word

Rules for Operatives (everybody else)

  1. Use your Codemaster's clue to figure out which words on the board are your codewords!
  2. If you choose the civilian's codewords or the opponent's codewords, your turn ends!
  3. If you choose the assassin's codeword, the other team wins!
